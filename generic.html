About Me

Skilled Data Analyst/SQL Developer Expertise in SQL, Python, PowerBI, Azure, DataBricks, Data-driven Insights Expert

My journey into the world of electronics and communication engineering began as a high school student fueled by a fascination for the magic of technology. This initial spark evolved into a profound passion during my undergraduate studies, where I delved into the intricacies of control theory, systems, and embedded systems. It was during this time that I recognized the pivotal role of Big Data in unlocking new dimensions of innovation and efficiency in the field. This realization prompted me to extend my research into cloud technology, exploring seamless interlinkages between data and the burgeoning realm of the Internet of Things (IoT). As my academic pursuits advanced into a Master's degree at the University of South Florida, I navigated towards a transformative course in data analytics, a turning point that shifted my focus to the vast possibilities within Big Data Analytics. Merging my expertise in embedded IoT with data analytics, I concentrated on energy data analytics, seeking to enhance the sustainability and efficiency of energy systems. This work not only honed my technical skills but also underscored the transformative potential of integrating machine learning with data analysis to address pressing energy challenges, solidifying my commitment to the realm of Big Data Analytics. Now standing at the threshold of a Ph.D., my passion for harnessing the power of Big Data remains unwavering. With a specific focus on optimizing energy consumption in smart grids through the convergence of IoT, machine learning, and Big Data Analytics, my journey is a testament to a relentless pursuit of knowledge and a commitment to using technology to address global challenges, particularly in the context of sustainable energy solutions.
